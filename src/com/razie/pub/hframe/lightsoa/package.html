Light SOA reflected wrapping of method calls

This package intends to eliminate much of the crap one has to deal with when writing any kind of service accessible remotely. There's a miriad of protocols today and each has its own quirks.

Just mark a method to be called with the @SoaMethod, listing the parameter names. Then wrap the object in a binding wrapper and register that with whatever protocol you need.

Currently, the parms are just Strings, but since they all get serialized somehow, what's the big deal?
